# Meet When

Meet When is an offline-first routine sharing app for BRACU students. Add your class routine, import friends' routines, and quickly find common free time without manually comparing schedules.

## Visit The Website

[Open Meet When](https://znydd.github.io/meet-when/)

## What It Does

- Saves your routine directly on your device
- Works as an installable PWA
- Imports routines from connect routine by copy-pasting
- Lets you add and manage friends' routines
- Suggests shared free time for meeting or planning together
- Keeps routine data local with offline storage, no login or sigh up

## Built For

Students who need a simple way to compare class schedules and decide when everyone is free.
